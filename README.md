### вңҰ About Me 

IвҖҷm a **Web & UX/UI designer + Front-End Developer** who loves creating modern, experimental visuals. With knowledge of HTML/CSS & an eye for interactive experiences, I design interfaces that are functional & accessible yet immersive вҖ” with playful touches like glassmorphism, dynamic gradients, & subtle motion that bring projects to life.  

вҖң**TaaraNova**вҖқ was born from a fascination with supernovas вҖ” cosmic bursts of light that mark both an ending & the beginning of something entirely new.  
Just like a starвҖҷs eruption creates the building blocks for new worlds, I see design as an ever-evolving process of transformation, experimentation, & renewal.  

The name plays on *terra nova* (вҖңnew worldвҖқ) & *supernova*, but with **taara (аӨӨаӨҫаӨ°аӨҫ/ШӘШ§ШұШ§)** вҖ” the Hindi/Urdu word for вҖңstarвҖқ вҖ” at its core, becoming вҖңnew star.вҖқ It reflects my cultural roots, curiosity for the unknown, & drive to create work that feels both luminous & grounded.  

Like the starburst behind the name, my career is just beginning вҖ” but IвҖҷm always pushing into bigger, brighter territory.
